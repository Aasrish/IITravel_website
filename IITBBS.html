Khurda Road Station

Khurda Road is an important railway junction station of Bhubaneswar in the Indian state of Odisha. It serves Jatani, which is also known as Khurda Road.

Distance from IIT Bhubaneswar: 8km

Biju Patnaik Airport

Biju Patnaik Airport (IATA: BBI, ICAO: VEBS), also known as the Bhubaneswar Airport, is the primary airport serving Bhubaneswar, the capital city of Odisha.

Distance from IIT Bhubaneswar: 35km
