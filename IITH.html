Lingampalli Railway Station

Lingampalli railway station is a railway station in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. Localities like BHEL Township and University of Hyderabad are accessible from this station.

Distance from IIT Hyderabad: 25km

Rajiv Gandhi International Airport

Rajiv Gandhi International Airport (IATA: HYD, ICAO: VOHS) is an international airport that serves Hyderabad, the capital of the Indian state of Telangana.

Distance from IIT Hyderabad: 60km
