Kanpur Central Railway Station

Kanpur Central (formerly known as Cawnpore North Barracks, station code: CNB) is a central and junction railway station in the city of Kanpur and is one of the five central Indian railway stations.

Distance from IIT Kanpur: 15km

Chaudhary Charan Singh International Airport

Chaudhary Charan Singh International Airport (IATA: LKO, ICAO: VILK) is an international airport serving Lucknow, the capital and the largest city of the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh.

Distance from IIT Kanpur: 95km
