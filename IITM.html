Chennai Central Railway Station

Puratchi Thalaivar Dr. M.G. Ramachandran Central Railway Station, commonly known as Chennai Central (station code: MAS), is the main railway terminus in the city of Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. It is the busiest railway station in South India and one of the most important hubs in the country.

Distance from IIT Madras: 14km

Chennai International Airport

Chennai International Airport (IATA: MAA, ICAO: VOMM) is an international airport serving the city of Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, and its metropolitan area. It is located in Meenambakkam, 21 km (13 mi) from the city centre. Chennai International Airport is the sixth busiest airport in India.

Distance from IIT Madras: 13km
